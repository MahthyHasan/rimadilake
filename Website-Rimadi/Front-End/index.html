Welcome to
Rimadi Lake Guest INN

Nestled in Eravur Town near Batticaloa City on Sri Lanka's East Coast, Rimadi Lake Guest Inn offers a serene escape amid nature. It overlooks Eravur Lagoon and lies close to the tourist attraction Lagoon Park. With over ten rooms available, guests can choose between AC and non-AC accommodations.

This tranquil inn in the heart of Eastern Province boasts a scenic location by Eravur Lagoon. Offering a variety of rooms—AC and non-AC—it's an ideal retreat. The proximity to Lagoon Park adds to its appeal for tourists seeking a peaceful stay.

Rimadi Lake Guest Inn, set in Eravur Town, Sri Lanka, is a nature-surrounded haven. Featuring diverse room options—AC and non-AC—guests are ensured a comfortable stay. Ample car parking and delicious meals further enhance the delightful experience for visitors.
